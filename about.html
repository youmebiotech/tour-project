WHO WE ARE?

Youme Tech Co., Ltd. (here in after referred to as Youme Tech) is a leading high-tech enterprise dedicated to pioneering research and development in the field of key raw materials for In-Vitro Diagnostic, Life Science, and Biomedical reagent solutions. Youme Tech, Bioeast Biotech has emerged as a trusted provider of innovative solutions in the industry. We specialize in developing advanced technologies and cutting-edge solutions to meet the evolving needs of the diagnostic, life science, and biomedical sectors. Our product portfolio encompasses a wide range of offerings, including Nano microspheres, magnetic beads, antigens, antibodies, enzymes, active proteins, chromatographic products, and integrated solutions for various diagnostic platforms such as Biochemistry, CLIA, Elisa, Lateral Flow, Fluroimmunoassay, Immunoturbidimetric, and Molecular Diagnostics.
